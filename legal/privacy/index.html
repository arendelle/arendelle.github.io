General Scope

This policy covers personally identifiable information collected or 
stored by Arendelle Project on its servers in relation to the Projects
and their communities. Consistent with its Data Retention Policy, the 
Project collects and retains the least amount of personally 
identifiable information needed to fulfill the Project's operational 
needs.
 


The Information We Collect

In our bug reporter website we only collect necessary information that 
recognizes you as a user. The only real data you need give us is your 
e-mail; In doing so, we use said data to get in touch with you 
therefore we need true and valid information. The collected data is 
not used for any other purposes and will not be shared by us. Said 
information will be emailed to the developers of the project and a 
temporary copy of email will be saved in the projects databases and 
will be erased within one week. There is no need for validity in any 
other information we ask of you. The Arendelle Project, Project owners
and contributers and other partners do not take any responsibilities 
in this field.


 
Cookies

Cookies are saved in your device. They allow for better and faster 
browsing and many of the sites features require cookies. Arendelle 
Project's website saves NO cookie on your system. 



Out Of Website Links

The thepa takes no responsibility in the links that are posted in our
sites. Each valid site has their own Privacy Policy and before using
these links you must read and accept said Privacy Policies.



Where your data is

The only type data we keep is bug report which will be kept safely on
our servers in Amazon's safe AWS servers in California with highest 
possible security.
