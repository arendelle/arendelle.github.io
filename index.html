More than a too easy language, Arendelle has a very simple environment. We took so much care to bring you the easiest, most natural experience in every app we created for Arendelle. Once you download the app you’re a master of it!










The main reason why Arendelle was invented was creating a LEGO tool/toy like system for creative people to play with creation. In order to make Arendelle a first-class language for creativity many careful designs have been done. For example, limits and restrictions make people be more creative. Arendelle is designed with some limits on purpose. You get only 5 colors. This may seem a bit strange but the 5 colors of Arendelle is one of it’s core characteristics. We ship Arendelle with a very rich set of built in mathematical functions. However many of the things you get in Arendelle are limited. To push the walls you have to code yourself. We always say that there is no language as creative as Arendelle but also is designed to make it’s users more creative than ever!
